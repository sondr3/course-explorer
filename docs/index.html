Course dependencies

The result of a question, can you visualize the dependencies/recommended courses at UiB? Turns out, yes! This visualization was hacked together quickly, you can hover over the blobs and see which course it is. The bigger the blob the more courses mention it. You can move around by pressing and dragging, and if you want to you can also click and drags the blobs around. If you want to show all courses press the button below the visualization, just beware that your PC will hate you.
